Senior Graphic Design Portfolio Show & Thesis Exhibition

After their time at the University of Minnesota's College of Design, 16 senior graphic designers are sharing their best work to date. Experience the conclusion of an educational journey that has been built on late nights, hard work, and a fading of the “command-z” keys. Each day in the program served as a single layer in the composition of their college career. You are invited to join the seniors as they put the final touches on their coursework and prepare to create a new layer of their lives.
